ArmaLac Metal Lacquer is a direct-to-metal clear coat suitable for commercial and industrial applications where protection of metal is required; architectural panelling, lighting, copper piping, taps, door furniture, household fittings, decorative components, ecclesiastical items, signage, metal artworks, alloy wheels and jewellery.

Purposeful

ArmaLac Metal Lacquer is a clear, fast-drying, acrylic lacquer which is designed to provide long-lasting protection against tarnishing, corrosion and oxidation of the substrate metal.

Innovative

Specialised surface chemistry and proprietary coating technology ensures ArmaLac Metal Lacquer achieves superior adhesion to the surface of the metal, including polished metals.

Versatile

ArmaLac Metal Lacquer is specifically designed as a clear coating for copper, brass and bronze but may also be used on other metals such as silver, zinc, silver, steel and aluminium.

Durable

ArmaLac Metal Lacquer has excellent UV and weathering resistance and is suitable for indoor and outdoor use.
